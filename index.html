Visit TamilNadu

Enjoy culture and sports in this vibrant South Indian state close to the Indian Ocean

Top 3 activities to do in TamilNadu

Enjoy the amazing art works

Tanjore Brihadeeswara temple was built 1000 years ago and art works and sculptures are amazing and mind blowing.

Take photos and enjoy.

Adi Yogi statue is one of the largest statues in India. It is a great place to take photos.

A bridge where train and ship can go.

Pamban bridge is amazing where train can go and under that the ships can go. Simply amazing work and give a visit and it is worth it.
